Market Notes Haven Protocol Handler Test

This page allows you to test the custom protocol handlers for the Market Notes Haven application.

How it works:

When you click on a protocol button, your browser will attempt to launch the application via its registered protocol handler. If the app is installed and properly registered these links should open the app.

For testing during development, you can register the protocol by:

  1. Installing the PWA
  2. Visiting chrome://apps in Chrome
  3. Right-clicking on the app and selecting "Open as window"
